Strategy: Engagement and Success for Elementary and Middle School Students

Annual Grant Funding: $34,000

Chapter 510’s Writing to Readiness program will support all 120 eighth graders at Westlake Middle School during the 2019-2020 school year with thrice-weekly, in-class writing tutoring, and will provide daily after-school literacy support services to those eighth graders who are not yet considered high school-ready. Our goal is to increase Westlake Middle School’s entire eighth grade class’ readiness to graduate and be successful in high school.
